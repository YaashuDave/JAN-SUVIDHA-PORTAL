Name: Manav Kalyan Yojana
About:

The Manav Kalyan Yojana of the Government of Gujarat is a scheme designed with the aim of economic progress and advancement of the backward caste and poor community. Under this scheme, financial help will be given to 28 (twenty eight) types of low income earners. The Government of Gujarat has declared Manav Kalyan Yojana 2022 to help those who are employed as hawkers, vegetable sellers, carpenters, washermen, cobblers etc.
Manav Kalyan Yojana was first announced by the Government of Gujarat on 11.09.1995 for the backward and poor community. An upgraded version of that scheme has been announced in 2022. Under this scheme, those artisans, laborers, small vendors etc. belonging to backward castes, whose income is up to Rs 12,000 in rural areas and up to Rs 15,000 in urban areas, will be given financial assistance by the government. Additional tools and equipment will also be provided to them under this scheme. The main objective of this scheme is to improve the economic condition of the artisans, laborers and small businessmen struggling with the economic condition and provide them self-employment opportunities.
This scheme has been issued by the Commissioner of Cottage and Rural Industries of the state government. In the previous years, the application forms for this scheme were filled through the offline process. But in the current year 2022, online applications will be accepted through e Samaj Kalyan Portal. Manav Kalyan Yojana Online 2022 Form can be filled at e-kutir Gujarat.

Eligiblity:

. The candidate must be a permanent resident of Gujarat.
. Their age should be within 16-60 years.
. Beneficiary Name Rural Development Dept. Must be in BPL List.
. They are required to show income certificate which is attested by Mamlatdar or any . responsible officer of Municipality or Municipal Corporation.
