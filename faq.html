FAQs( Frequently Asked Questions )

Q1.WHo can register for Jan Suvidha Portal ?

Ans1.Anyone having a shop with Laptop,Printer and internet connection nad lives anywhere in India

Q2.After registering how long does it take for registration id to be generated ?

Ans2.After registering on the portal , the documents provided by you are verified and post verification your id gets activated and this process takes from 2-24 hours.

Q3.What are the different ways available to recharge a wallet on Jan Suvidha Portal ?

Ans3.For the wallet recharge, you can use internet banking or BHIM UPI.

Q4.WHat are the services provided by JAN Suvidha Portal ?

Ans4.This portal provides you services like schemes related to Agriculture,Health,Rural development etc.

Q5.Is there any cost incurred for taking Jan Suvidha Portal Franchise ?

Ans5.There is no cost associated to take the franchise for the Jan Suvidha Portal.
